[Ubuntu 26.04](https://documentation.ubuntu.com/release-notes/26.04/) was released last week and we have supported it since the middle of March!

[Since the 12th of March](https://voxpupuli.org/blog/2026/02/17/new-openvox-agent-8.25.0-releases/), we offer Ubuntu 26.04 OpenVox-Agent packages, for amd64, aarch64 and armhf.
And with the next OpenVoxDB & OpenVox-Server release we will also offer Ubuntu 26.04 packages, the [RC packages are already available](https://voxpupuli.org/blog/2026/04/24/new-openvox-server-and-db-releases/).

After two little changes to [beaker-hostgenerator](https://github.com/voxpupuli/beaker-hostgenerator/pull/463) and [puppet_metadata](https://github.com/voxpupuli/puppet_metadata/pull/228), we can now also test all our modules against Ubuntu 26.04 with acceptance tests.
And with one change in [facterdb](https://github.com/voxpupuli/facterdb/pull/453) we added support for unit testing.

All that's required to test modules against Ubuntu 26.04 is adding 26.04 to the metadata.json.
