The **Vox Pupuli PMC Election 2026 has officially concluded**.

We are pleased to announce the newly elected members of the Project Management Committee (PMC), who will help guide the Vox Pupuli project over the coming year.

---

## Elected PMC Members

The following candidates are elected to the PMC:

- Tim Meusel  
- Sebastian Rakel  
- Ben Ford  
- Nick Burgan  
- Robert Waffen  
- Romain Tartière  
- Kenyon Ralph  

Congratulations to all elected members!

[Result](https://civs1.civs.us/cgi-bin/results.pl?id=E_c04b15d1022de02c)

---

## Acknowledgements

We would like to sincerely thank **all candidates** for their willingness to step forward and contribute to the community.  
Your engagement and commitment are essential to the continued success of Vox Pupuli.

A big thank you also goes to the **entire community** for participating in the voting process and helping shape the future of the project.

---

## Closing the Election

As the Election Officer, I hereby officially close the Vox Pupuli PMC Election 2026.

We are looking forward to another great year of collaboration, innovation, and community growth.

Thank you all for being part of Vox Pupuli.
