There will be another VoxConf! As announced in Gent, we will do another conference this year, 16th of July in Garching (Germany).

We want to run our own conference every year now. As last time, we want to partner with the Foreman community.
VoxConf will happen mid-morning, the Foreman Birthday Party in the afternoon.
[ATIX AG](https://atix.de/en/) will sponsor the location this year, next year [betadots GmbH](https://www.betadots.de/) will provide a location in Berlin, Germany.

We updated [our conference page](https://voxpupuli.org/voxconf/) and created a dedicated page [for this year](https://voxpupuli.org/voxconf/2026/).

Please checkout the page, send us your talk proposals, let us know if you need travel funding!
