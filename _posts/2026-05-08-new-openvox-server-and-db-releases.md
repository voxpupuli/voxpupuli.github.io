This week we released OpenVox-Server 8.13.0 & OpenVoxDB 8.13.0!

We initially announced it [in our previous blog post](https://voxpupuli.org/blog/2026/04/24/new-openvox-server-and-db-releases/), it also contains the changelog.

The new packages, including FIPS builds for EL, and Ubuntu 26.04 packages, are available on our official mirrors [apt.voxpupuli.org](https://apt.voxpupuli.org/) / [https://yum.voxpupuli.org/](https://yum.voxpupuli.org/).

If you find any bugs or want to contribute, please come to our [#openvox](https://voxpupuli.org/connect/) channel on IRC or slack.
