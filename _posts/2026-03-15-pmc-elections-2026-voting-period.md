# Vox Pupuli PMC Election 2026 – Voting Phase Started

The **voting phase for the Vox Pupuli PMC Election 2026 has officially started**.

Community members are invited to participate and vote for the next 7 members of the Project Management Committee (PMC).  
The PMC helps guide the Vox Pupuli project, coordinates community work, and supports the long-term growth of the community.

**Voting closes on March 29, 2026 at 19:00 UTC.**

Please make sure to submit your vote before the deadline.
