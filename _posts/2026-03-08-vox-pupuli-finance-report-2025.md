We want to provide a report about all spendings and contributions from 2025!

How went 2025 for Vox Pupuli, from a financial point of view?

As a brief reminder, we accept donations via [open-collective](https://opencollective.com/vox-pupuli) and [GitHub sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/voxpupuli).
GitHub sponsors money is forwarded to open-collective.
Both organisations take a fee for processing funding, so we prefer open-collective.

### In 2025

* We received $5,895 via GitHub Sponsors at open-collective
* We received $14,450 directly at open-collective
* we made a profit of $10,052 in 2025
* We paid $2,285 for processing fees
* We spent $8,006

### Overall

* We raised $35,775
* We have $23,434 on the bank account
* We disbursed $12,341

**A big thank you to [saz](https://github.com/saz/) for being our first contributor in February 2023**
